About Us

Foundation Cry is a social welfare foundation dedicated to supporting underprivileged communities, providing education, healthcare, and livelihood opportunities. We believe in the power of collective action to bring about meaningful change in society. CRY works with project partners at the grassroots level to create sustainable change for India’s children.

Our approach

Our approach is modeled around bringing change at all levels With your support, we address children’s critical needs by working with parents, teachers, Anganwadi workers, communities, district and state level governments as well as the children themselves. We focus on changing behaviours and practices at the grassroots level and influencing public policy at a systemic level – thereby creating an ecosystem where children are made the nation’s priority.

Our vision

A happy, healthy and creative child whose rights are protected and honoured in a society that is built on respect for dignity, justice and equity for all.
